Full Program Details

Our Winter Karate Clinic is designed to enhance your skills, build confidence, and ignite your passion for Karate.

Participants will be grouped by skill level and rotated through a variety of activities, learning from expert instructors every 25 minutes.

Over the course of this three-hour camp, you'll experience unique perspectives on our beautiful art form, sharpening techniques and breathing the spirit of Karate in an energizing and supportive environment.

Our Karate Camp is part of an ongoing series of meet-ups, giving you regular opportunities to grow, connect with fellow enthusiasts, and deepen your practice.
